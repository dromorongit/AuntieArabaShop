About Us

Discover the story behind Shop with Auntie Araba and our passion for fashion

Our Story

Welcome to Shop with Auntie Araba, your premier destination for premium ladies fashion in Ghana. Founded in 2025, we have quickly established ourselves as a trusted name in the fashion industry, catering to the modern woman's style needs.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to provide high-quality, stylish, and affordable fashion that empowers women to express their unique personalities through clothing. We believe that every woman deserves to feel confident and beautiful, and that's exactly what our carefully curated collection helps achieve.

From elegant dresses to comfortable night wear, from trendy crop tops to professional office attire, we offer a comprehensive range of fashion items that cater to every aspect of a woman's wardrobe. Our commitment to quality and style has made us a favorite among fashion-conscious women across Ghana.

Our Mission

To empower women through fashion by providing high-quality, stylish, and affordable clothing that enhances their confidence and allows them to express their unique personalities. We strive to be the go-to destination for ladies fashion in Ghana and beyond.

Our Vision

To be the leading fashion retailer in West Africa, known for our commitment to quality, style, and customer satisfaction. We envision a future where every woman has access to fashion that makes her feel beautiful and confident.

Our Values

Quality

We are committed to providing only the highest quality products that meet our stringent standards for style, comfort, and durability.

Style

Our team of fashion experts carefully selects each item to ensure it meets the latest trends while maintaining timeless appeal.

Customer First

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. Their satisfaction and happiness are our top priorities.

Integrity

We believe in honest business practices, fair pricing, and transparent communication with our valued customers.

Meet Our Founder

Mary Diamond

Founder & CEO

Mary Diamond, the visionary behind Shop with Auntie Araba, has always been passionate about fashion and empowering women. With years of experience in the fashion industry, she founded Shop with Auntie Araba with the goal of making stylish, high-quality fashion accessible to every woman in Ghana and beyond.

Her dedication to quality, style, and customer satisfaction has been the driving force behind the shop's rapid growth and success. Mary's mission is to ensure that every woman feels confident and beautiful in the clothes she wears.
